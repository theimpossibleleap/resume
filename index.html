About

Hello,

Thank you for taking the time to read my resume. I really appreciate it! I know it's hard to capture a person's experience, skills, and personality in a traditional resume. Because of that, I decided to make this website. It has most the information you may need, as well as some you absolutely do not. This includes some Easter eggs hidden in links. Click around!

I currently live in Las Vegas, NV. Unlike most people, I was born and raised here! I have a deep passion for cooking, baking, coffee, technology, travel, and carpentry. I'm a very curious person! Whenever I hear something is challenging to do, I want to try and master it! This includes baking Macarons, to remodeling my entire kitchen myself. All it takes some times is blood, sweat, Google, and YouTube. I'm currently deep in to A-Frame cabin building.. I'll try and make that happen soon!

Technology has been a huge part of my life since I was a kid. My neighbor taught me how to run Doom through a command prompt, on a floppy disk, in 1994. Ever since then I've been interested in computers, technology, and learning how it all works. I started building websites in the late 90s / early 2000s. It was a lot of copy and pasting.. There was also a lot of Geocities goofiness, if you remember that.. I'm glad I stuck with it though! I'm currently going to school for my Bachelor of Science in Software Development. I'm currently a Manager with Apple Retail. It has truly been an amazing experience. I'm excited for the next chapter in my career as I pursue positions more relevant to my learning and future growth!

I want to note that my website was 100% developed and coded by myself. With a little help from Google and MDN Docs. I built it to be responsive, have quick load times, and be flexible to my needs. I hope that comes across, and I hope you enjoy!
